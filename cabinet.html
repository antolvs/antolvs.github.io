Custom Cabinet

Custom arcade cabinets! A common project within the Raspberry Pi community, and a fun one at that.

Using a first generation Arcade1Up Asteroids cabinet as a base for this project, I cleared out all of the internals (LCD board, control panel, all wiring) except for the stock monitor that came with it.

The stock LCD board contains everything the cabinet needed to function. The sounds, visuals, and games. I needed to replace this board with my own LCD board that can support a Raspberry Pi connection and custom speakers.

Removing the stock LCD board was as simple as four screws, and installing the new board was even easier.

This is the cabinet as it currently stands to date (as of 07-06-2024).

The games are fully functional, however the only games I cared to ensure that works as intended is TRON and Discs of TRON. The spinner on the left works flawlessly and features a custom TRON finish, the spin-ball in the middle also doubles as a control for the spinner/cursor, and the TRON joystick works and looks perfect. all buttons work aside from the bottom left 3 buttons, which I assume is a wiring issue on my end.

The project won't be "completed" in my eyes until I clean up the wiring on the inside and wrap the cabinet in custom TRON artwork, which is hopefully soon.
